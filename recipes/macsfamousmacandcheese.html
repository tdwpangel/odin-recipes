Macs' Famous Mac and Cheese

A very tasty and cheesy take on Mac and Cheese made famous by Ronald "Mac" McDonald

Macs' Famous Mac and Cheese is a dish made famous by Mac from the show It's Always Sunny In Philadelphia, and is Dennis's Favorite meal to eat.

Ingredients

  • Boxed Mac And Cheese
  • 6 Cups Water
  • 1/4 Cup Milk
  • 1/4 Cup Butter
  • 1 Pound Meat Hunks (Dennis)

Steps

  1. Bring water to boil in medium saucepan. Add Macaroni; cook 7 to 8 min. or until tender, stirring occasionally.
  2. Drain. (Do not rinse.) Return macaroni to pan.
  3. Prepare Dennis hunks....Dennis likes Dennis!
  4. Add margarine, milk and Cheese Sauce Mix to macaroni; mix well.
  5. Toss in Meat Hunks and stir
  6. Serve in cheap dishware or paper plate and enjoy!
